      The Ho Chi Minh City Museum, with its timeless Indochine architecture, already carried a quiet elegance of its own. Together with the Home Garden team, we suggested a style of décor that would complement — not overpower — the space: simple, graceful, and true to the spirit of time the museum holds.

      For the ceremony, the old flagpole and the canopy of trees became the backdrop — a subtle way to reflect Tess & Andy’s love for Vietnam. To weave in even more local charm, each dining table was named after one of the couple’s favourite streets in the city.

      And we’ll never forget the delighted looks and bright smiles of their international friends as they stepped into this space — a gentle meeting point of cultures, crafted with love by us, Tess & Andy.
