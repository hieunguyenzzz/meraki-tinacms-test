## Boundless Amour

**Nationality:** New Zealand - England  
**Guest Count:** 70  
**Type of Wedding:** Destination Wedding  

### What We Love

Tess and Andy have been preparing for the wedding for a long time before meeting with us. They came up with many awesome ideas and specific requirements; and we definitely loved it so much. Almost everything, from the furniture, decoration, food, beverage, lighting and sound, was separately ordered and delivered to the museum the day before the wedding.

Both Tess and Andy love Vietnam fervently; hence we brought the local characters into every little detail of the wedding, from the music to the beverage and cuisine. Andy even used a pho cooking pot as an ice bucket to cool up the beers, which was a fantastic idea and it perfectly added a local touch to the party concept.

### The Location

**Ho Chi Minh Museum**

This was the first wedding in Vietnam to be held at the Ho Chi Minh Museum. 

Holding a wedding in a public space was a big challenge because Ho Chi Minh Museum still opened to the public and welcomed delegations on the wedding day. But from this, we have learnt many great things including how to keep the wedding private so that everybody can fully enjoy the party without the influence from the general operation of the building.
