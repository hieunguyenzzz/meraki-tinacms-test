    description_en: >
      Both Linh and Chương have spent many years away from home. Away not only
      from familiar walls, but from childhood memories, from special treats
      saved for meaningful occasions, from rows of flowers glowing with
      chrysanthemums, lotuses, marigolds, and banana blossoms. For them, we
      created an intimate family ceremony that felt like a warm return.


      Linh’s home was filled with traditional sweets, bánh ống, bánh in, lotus
      jam, banana candy, along with tin cans tied behind the car and generous
      bouquets scattered throughout the house. A sense of closeness lived
      quietly in the orchids woven into many details, carrying a tender message
      that Linh’s mother would walk beside her, step by step, to the ceremony
      and throughout the journey ahead.
    description_vi: >
