  - description_en: |
      For their wedding, Daphne & Patrick chose a palette inspired by nature itself. Soft, understated tones blended seamlessly with the untouched beauty of Vinh Hy while reflecting their own gentle and refined personalities.

      Bamboo became the defining material throughout both the Tea Ceremony and the wedding ceremony. In many Asian cultures, bamboo symbolizes resilience, prosperity, and enduring happiness, carrying with it hopes for a future filled with joy and good fortune.

      The ceremony arch was crafted from layers of curved bamboo strips woven together to resemble two bird nests leaning gently toward one another, a poetic reflection of Daphne & Patrick’s love story. Along the aisle, clusters of bamboo guided the way as Daphne walked beside her parents toward the ceremony space, creating an atmosphere that felt both serene and deeply emotional.

      Nearly every corner of the celebration carried subtle references to Asian culture, a heartfelt tribute to the couple’s roots and heritage. The evening reception, however, embraced a different perspective. Inspired by Western aesthetics, it became a thoughtful gesture of appreciation toward the many friends who had traveled from afar to celebrate with them.

      Curved dining tables softened the space, while delicate floral-shaped pendant lights shimmered overhead. Together with carefully chosen details, they created an atmosphere that felt intimate, elegant, and quietly romantic.
