      Xu had always dreamed of getting married in the middle of a forest, surrounded only by the people closest to her heart. After spending a long time searching, she finally found a hidden pine forest nestled within Da Lat, the very city where Hiếu first confessed his love to her.

      Beneath the towering pine trees, the team at HOME GARDEN brought one of the bride’s most whimsical wishes to life: “We want flowers falling over our heads.” A suspended floral installation was carefully created to resemble blossoms drifting through the forest air, becoming one of the most unforgettable visual details of the ceremony.
