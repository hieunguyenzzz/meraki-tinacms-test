    description_en: |
      For us, weaving multiple cultures into a single wedding is both a challenge and a privilege. To ensure that every guest felt included, all vows, speeches, and family messages were carefully translated into three languages: English, Chinese, and Vietnamese. Communication, after all, is one of the purest forms of care.

      Beyond visual storytelling, Jamie & Jeremy’s wedding engaged the senses in thoughtful ways. The sound of Vietnamese traditional instruments, the rhythmic drums announcing the lion dance as the couple entered the reception, and the refined Western cuisine meticulously prepared by the Park Hyatt Hotel all came together to create a layered, immersive experience.

      Above all, this wedding will always remain in our hearts as a portrait of harmony — of love shaped by understanding, and of deep respect for culture and origin. Jamie & Jeremy reminded us that when differences are embraced with sincerity, they do not divide; they enrich, elevate, and endure.
