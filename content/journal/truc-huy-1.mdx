      After many conversations with Huy & Trúc, we found ourselves thinking of one sweet concept: soulmates. Not only because of the way they love each other, but because of the way they listen, respect, and remain patient with one another every day. Their understanding of each other is not something that happened by chance. It has been nurtured through countless heartfelt conversations and meaningful moments. That very spirit inspired us to create a wedding day that felt warm and romantic, where everything began with harmony and connection.

Không chỉ bởi cách họ yêu nhau, mà còn bởi cách họ lắng nghe, tôn trọng và kiên nhẫn với nhau mỗi ngày.   - description_en: |
      The ceremony took place by the sea, where gentle breezes and soft sunlight cast a dreamy filter over the landscape, making even the transparent chairs glow with pastel hues. Beneath the swaying palm trees, fresh coconuts printed with Huy & Trúc’s names were served at every table, creating a feeling that was simple and refreshing. As night fell, the summer garden remained luminous beneath strings of twinkling lights that resembled stars in the sky. A photobooth was also thoughtfully placed nearby, becoming a corner where guests could capture and preserve precious moments with friends.
