      There was something quietly magical about how Giang and Hiệp found one another — the kind of meeting that reads like the opening line of a favorite story. Their paths first crossed in the hush of a church when they were still teenagers, and from that first glance a memory took root: a young man carrying the image of a girl through years and across oceans. Even when half the world stretched between them, they chose to be together and tended to that choice every single day. We felt deeply privileged to play a small part in weaving the next chapter of their sweet, steadfast love — to help write their “Happy Ever After.”
