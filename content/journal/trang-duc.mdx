      There are weddings that follow a careful script - and then there are the ones that live on because of the moments that arrive unannounced. The Phú Quốc wind didn’t derail the day - it nudged Meraki and the couple toward a different, delightfully intimate journey.

      As if making up for the storms that had just passed, the sky over Phú Quốc that evening blazed with a fiery sunset. Against the golden sand and warm afternoon light, the floral arrangements were kept minimal with soft white and pastel pink blooms, appearing even more radiant beneath the glowing sky. Đức made his entrance down the aisle alongside a lively dance performance from the bridal party. With flower petals tucked neatly into small side pouches, the bridesmaids and groomsmen scattered them into the air with each step of the choreography. The playful yet impressive “grand entrance” quickly became a highlight of the ceremony, leaving guests thoroughly entertained and admiring a bridal party truly worth every bit of the hype.
