  - description_en: |
      If the ceremony was a graceful classical composition, then the reception became a spectacular stage where contemporary music came fully to life. The powerful sound of electric guitar intertwined with a Jazz band, setting the tone for Vân Anh & Mark’s very own music festival: #BURNINGNAM2023.

      Making their entrance alongside a traditional lion dance, a vibrant symbol of cultural heritage, Vân Anh and Mark instantly ignited the celebratory spirit of the evening. The night continued to build with remarkable performances, from powerful vocals and captivating DJ sets to energetic saxophone melodies and spontaneous mixtapes curated by the groom himself.
