    description_en: |
      From the beginning, Vanessa & Tùng Anh envisioned a celebration set within an open landscape, somewhere immersed in nature and quietly removed from the city. The Campville eventually stood out for its hillside framed by pine trees, wild grasses, and one of the most beautiful sunsets in Đà Lạt.

      What made the venue special, however, was also what made it challenging. Unlike a conventional wedding venue, The Campville offered a blank canvas where nearly everything had to be built from the ground up. From the ceremony lawn and reception space to the kitchen, power supply, and guest experience, every element required careful planning long before the wedding day arrived.

      In the days leading up to the wedding, relentless rain completely transformed the hillside. The ground softened, the landscape shifted, and plans changed almost by the hour. Yet standing there that evening, it was hard to imagine everything had once existed only as sketches and ideas.
